# Canva Studio

A free, AI-powered graphics editor that runs in your browser. Create social media posts, presentations, posters, and more — no software to install.

**Live app:** https://arieskalalo.github.io/canva-studio/

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account
- Open the app and click **Sign Up**
- Enter your name, email, and a password
- Check your email for a confirmation link, then sign in

### 2. Create Your First Design
Once signed in, you'll land on your **Dashboard**.

Choose a quick-start size or click **New Design** to set a custom size:

| Template | Size |
|---|---|
| Instagram Post | 1080 × 1080 px |
| Presentation | 1920 × 1080 px |
| Facebook Cover | 1200 × 628 px |
| Story | 1080 × 1920 px |
| Custom | Any size you want |

## Working With Objects

### Adding Objects
- **Shapes:** Select the shape tool, then click and drag on the canvas
- **Text:** Select the Text tool, click on the canvas, then type. Double-click any text to edit it
- **Images:** Select the Image tool to upload from your computer

### Selecting & Moving
- Click any object to select it
- Drag it to move
- Drag the corner handles to resize
- Drag the rotation handle (top) to rotate
- Hold `Shift` and click to select multiple objects

### Editing Properties (Right Panel)
When an object is selected, the right panel shows its properties:

**For all objects:**
- Position (X, Y) and size (W, H)
- Rotation angle
- Opacity slider

**For shapes:**
- Fill color
- Stroke (border) color and width

**For text:**
- Font family
- Font size
- Text color
- Bold / Italic / Underline
- Alignment (left, center, right)
- Line height

**For images:**
- Remove Background button (AI-powered, runs in your browser)

### Arranging Objects
Use the arrange buttons at the bottom of the right panel:

| Button | Action |
|---|---|
| Bring to Front | Move object above all others |
| Send to Back | Move object behind all others |
| Flip H | Flip horizontally |
| Flip V | Flip vertically |
| Trash | Delete selected object |

## Layers Panel

At the bottom of the screen is the **Layers Panel**. Click the arrow to expand it.

- Every object on the canvas appears as a layer
- Click a layer to select that object
- Use the **up/down arrows** to reorder layers
- Click the **X** to delete a layer

## AI Features

Click the **AI** button in the left toolbar to open the AI Assistant. It has three tabs:

### Layout Generator
Describe your design in plain English and AI will build it for you.

**Example prompts:**
- *"A bold Instagram post for a coffee shop with a dark background, large white headline, and a call-to-action button"*
- *"A clean minimal presentation slide with a title and three bullet points"*
- *"A colorful birthday flyer with confetti colors and big text"*

Click **Generate Layout** — the AI will place shapes, text, and colors directly onto your canvas.

### Copywriter
Generate professional text for your design.

1. Enter your **brand or topic** (e.g. "coffee shop", "fitness app")
2. Choose a **tone** (Professional, Casual, Bold, Playful, Luxury)
3. Choose the **type** of copy (Headline, Tagline, Call-to-Action, Description, Social Caption)
4. Click **Generate Copy** — you'll get 3 options
5. Click any option to add it to your canvas

### Image Generator
Generate AI images for free (powered by Pollinations.ai — no account needed).

1. Describe the image you want
2. Choose a style (Photorealistic, Digital Art, Watercolor, Oil Painting, 3D Render, Flat Illustration, Sketch)
3. Click **Generate Image**
4. Click **Add to Canvas** to place it in your design

> Images generate in about 10–20 seconds. Try to be descriptive for better results.

## Background Removal

Select any image on the canvas, then look for **Remove Background** in the right panel.

- Runs entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server
- Works on photos, product images, logos, and more
- Processing takes 5–15 seconds depending on your device

---

## Brand Kit

Click the **Brand** button to open your Brand Kit. This stores your brand's colors, fonts, and logo so you can apply them instantly to any design.

### Setting up your Brand Kit
1. Click **+ Add Color** and pick your brand colors
2. Choose fonts from the dropdown and click **+ Add Font**
3. Upload your logo with **Upload Logo**
4. Click **Save Brand Kit** — it's stored in your account

### Using your Brand Kit
- Click any color swatch to apply it to the selected object
- Click any font to apply it to selected text
- Click **Add to Canvas** to place your logo on the design

---

## Design Versioning (Snapshots)

Click the **history icon** (top right) to open Design History. This lets you save named snapshots of your design at any point.

**How to save a snapshot:**
1. Type a name (e.g. "Client Review v1", "Before changes")
2. Click **Save Snapshot**

**How to restore a snapshot:**
- Click **Restore** next to any saved snapshot
- Your canvas will revert to that version

> Your design also auto-saves every 2 minutes.

---

## Smart Resize

Click **Resize** in the top bar to scale your design to a different format.

Choose a preset (Instagram, Presentation, Story, Facebook, A4) or enter a custom width and height. All objects are repositioned proportionally — nothing gets cut off.

---

## Batch Variant Generator

Click the **Batch** button to generate multiple versions of your design at once. Great for A/B testing ads or creating posts for multiple campaigns.

1. Enter **headline variants** (comma-separated)
   - Example: `Summer Sale, Winter Deals, Spring Promo`
2. Enter **background color variants** (comma-separated hex)
   - Example: `#FF5733, #3498DB, #2ECC71`
3. Choose to **download as PNG** or **save to your designs**
4. Click **Generate Variants**

Each combination generates a separate design.

## Mockup Wrapper

Click the **Mockup** button to place your design inside a device or frame.

Available mockups:
- Phone
- Tablet
- Laptop
- Billboard
- T-Shirt
- Poster

Select one and click **Apply Mockup** — your design gets placed inside the frame as a grouped object.

## Exporting Your Design

Click the **Export** button (top right) and choose your format:

| Format | Best for |
|---|---|
| **PNG** | Social media, web, transparent backgrounds |
| **JPG** | Photos, smaller file sizes |
| **SVG** | Logos, icons, scalable graphics |
| **PDF** | Print, documents, presentations |
| **Send to Vidspark** | Animate your design as a video in Vidspark |

## Saving

- Click **Save** (top right) or press `Ctrl + S` at any time
- Your design auto-saves every 2 minutes
- Thumbnail previews are updated on each save and appear on your dashboard

---

## Tips & Tricks

- **Zoom in/out** using the zoom dropdown in the top bar (25% – 200%)
- **Double-click** any text object to enter edit mode
- **Hold Shift** while resizing to maintain aspect ratio (on most objects)
- Use **Undo (Ctrl+Z)** freely — up to 50 steps are stored per session
- **Duplicate** a design from the dashboard by hovering over it and clicking the copy icon
- The **Layers panel** is great for clicking hard-to-select objects hidden behind others
